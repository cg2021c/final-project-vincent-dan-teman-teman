Press S
Press S to start the game. The objective of the game is to avoid crashing with other vehicles.
The controls are :
  • S Key - Accelerate
  • D Key - Decelerate
  • Right Arrow - Move Camera to the Right
  • Left Arrow - Move Camera to the left
  • Up Arrow - Move Camera Upwards
  • Down Arrow - Move Camera Downwards
  • O Key - Lift Camera Height
  • P Key - Reduce Camera Height

    • You hit another vehicle

    To reset the game press R
